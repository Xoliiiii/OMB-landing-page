Overview

Dive into the vibrant world of South Central Los Angeles with our gripping coming-of-age series. Follow four street-smart friends as they navigate high school, battling not only academic challenges but also the harsh realities of life. Amidst the backdrop of a gritty community, witness friendships evolve into something deeper while tackling love, family, and the desperate attempt to rescue a friend from a dangerous gang. This series is a raw and authentic portrayal of adolescence, a rollercoaster of emotions that unveils the intricate mosaic of their lives. Get ready for a captivating journey through the heart of South Central LA.

CHARACTERS


MONSE FINNE

Monse is a headstrong, bossy girl of Afro-Latina descent. She was raised by her single father. Monse is a selfless, outspoken girl who will do anything to keep her friends together. She will always put the needs of others before her own.


CESAR DIAZ

Cesar is an intelligent teen forced into gang life by his brother Spooky once he was released from prison. He is a natural leader who is very caring toward his friends. He is shown to be very laid back and very easygoing. He is described by Monse as "the glue of our crew".


RUBEN MARTINEZ

Ruby is driven, fast-talking and self-centred. He has aspirations for himself and works hard to achieve his goals. At his best, Ruby starts to put his friends feelings before his own and is more helpful towards their desires.


JAMAL TURNER

Jamal is a quirky and loud boy and he is very funny. He can't keep a secret, hates football and doesn't want to disappoint his parents. In season 1, he is drawn into the mystery surrounding RollerWorld. He is the "believer" of the group.
